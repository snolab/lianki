## Research Insights

### Common Themes Across Top Content:
1. **Environment is crucial** - Immersion and natural exposure beat classroom study
2. **Sentence-based learning** - Learn language in context, not isolated words
3. **Consistent input** - Large amounts of comprehensible input (2000+ hours)
4. **Integration with interests** - Connect language learning with hobbies/passions
5. **Native speaker interaction** - Real conversations are essential
6. **Mindset over method** - Removing fear and treating language as a tool

### Top Methods Mentioned:
- **Immersive listening** (2000+ hours of comprehensible input)
- **Dictation and transcription** exercises
- **Sentence mining** from native content
- **Mother tongue audio + target language subtitles**
- **Wikipedia deep-dive** (understanding linguistic concepts)
- **Living in target language country**
- **Daily conversation with native speakers**
